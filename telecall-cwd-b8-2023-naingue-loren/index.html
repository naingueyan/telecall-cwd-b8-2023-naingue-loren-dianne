ABOUT

VIP Vape Shop is a premier destination for vaping enthusiasts in the heart of the city. Our lounge offers a welcoming and comfortable space for those looking to enjoy a wide variety of e-liquids, flavors, and devices in a relaxed, social atmosphere. Whether you're a seasoned vaper or just starting out, The Vip Lounge is the place to be.


We promote responsible vaping and kindly ask patrons to be considerate of others. VIP Vape Shop is a non-smoking establishment, and vaping is only allowed in designated areas. Please respect fellow patrons by not clouding up the lounge excessively. Minors (under 18) are not permitted in the lounge.


Promoting responsible smoking is essential for the well-being of both individuals and the community. As a responsible tobacco retailer, we are committed to providing our customers with information and resources to make informed choices about their smoking habits.
